TRAVELING

Within this book of traveling hacks, you will discover: Ways to travel even if you are broke Secrets to traveling the world on a very tight budget How to get amazing travel deals Strategies to travel the world's most expensive destinations

As much a guide as a travelogue, How to Travel the World for Free will give readers ideas for alternative ways of traveling and will inspire many to go on new adventures. This book is full of surprises, some more pleasant than others
